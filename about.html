PEEL ABOUT

This illustrated memoir chronicles the life of a second-generation Chinese-American. Childhood artifacts have been collected and refurbished into the narrative, which revolves around the intersections of family, culture, art, and identity.

About the Author

Austin Chin will be graduating from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in May 2017 with a dual BS in Electronic Arts and Computer Science. He is interested in the intersections of art, technology, and people.
More work can be found on his website.
